> "I've been comparing quotes:

> - Progressive quoted $142/month
> - USAA is at $115/month (if I qualify)
> All for the same coverage limits. I'm not shopping to leave, but I need competitive pricing."

**IDENTIFY DISCOUNTS:**

> - Usage-based Snapshot discount?"

**SPECIFIC ASK:**

**If they say:** "Your rate is based on your driving record/age/location"
**You respond:** "I understand the factors. Are there any changes I can make—like updating my annual mileage estimate or bundling policies—that would lower my rate?"

**TIMING:** Call Tuesday-Wednesday, early morning (before 10am) when retention specialists are focused
**DEPARTMENT:** Loyalty/Retention Department
**PREP:** Have competitor quotes ready (screenshots help)

**OPENING STATEMENT:**
> "Hi, I've been a Comcast customer for [X time], and I'm happy with the service, but my internet-only bill is now $89.99/month. I checked with AT&T Fiber and Spectrum, and they're offering 300+ Mbps for $45-65/month. Before I switch, what can you do to keep my business?"

**WHAT TO SAY (Key phrases):**
> "I understand you have promotional pricing. What's your best offer for a new 12-month promotion? I'm open to a 12-24 month contract if the price is right."

**PRESENT ALTERNATIVES:**
> "Here are my options:
> - AT&T Fiber: $49.99/month (12 months)
> - Spectrum: $65/month (1 year)
> - Stay with Comcast at $89.99/month
> These are all similar speeds (300+ Mbps). What's your lowest offer?"

**IF THEY SAY:** "That price is only for new customers"
**YOU RESPOND:**
> "I understand. As a loyal customer, can I be treated like a new customer? I could technically switch to AT&T and come back. But I'd rather keep Comcast if you can make it competitive. What's possible?"

**SPECIFIC ASK:**
> "Here's my request: Can you get me to $59.99/month for 12 months? If yes, I'll stay. If not, what's your absolute lowest offer?"

**BUNDLE MENTION:**
> "If I add [phone/mobile] service, does that unlock promotional pricing? What's the bundled rate?"

**OBJECTION HANDLING:**

**If they say:** "I can only offer $75/month" (common first offer)
**You respond:** "I appreciate that, but that's still $25 more than Fiber. Can you go lower? Or extend the discount to 24 months to make up the difference?"

**If they say:** "We can do $65/month, but only for 6 months"
**You respond:** "That's better, thank you. Can we do 12 months? And what happens at month 7—does it jump back to $89.99?"

**If they say:** "Xfinity Mobile could save you money overall"
**You respond:** "I'm open to that if it beats what I'm currently spending on [current provider]. What's the all-in price?"

**If they say:** "I can't match those prices; they're not accurate"
**You respond:** "I have them quoted in writing. Let me transfer you the link. [pause] These are definitely real. So where can you get?"

**NEGOTIATION FLOW:**

1. **First Ask:** $59.99/month for 12 months
2. **Counter (if denied):** $65/month for 12 months OR $59.99 for 6 months
3. **Final Position:** $65/month minimum; otherwise switch
4. **Walk Away:** Actually switch to AT&T (real threat)

**CLOSING:**
> "Perfect. So that's [confirm rate], for [X months], starting immediately. Can you email me the confirmation and rate guarantee? And what happens after [month X]—do I need to renegotiate?"

**Document this in writing.** Comcast is notorious for "technical errors" on bills.

**ACTION 1: Apply for CARE Program (California Alternate Rates for Energy)**
- **Eligibility:** Household income < 200-250% of federal poverty line
- **Savings:** 15-35% reduction on rates
- **How:** Call PG&E at 1-877-743-2273 and ask about CARE/LIHEAP programs
- **Time:** 15 minutes

**IF ELIGIBLE:** Automatic 20-30% reduction = **$29-43/month savings on PG&E alone**

**ACTION 2: Request Energy Audit (Free)**
> "Hi PG&E, I'm looking to reduce my electricity usage. Do you offer free energy audits or home assessments? I'd like to identify efficiency improvements."

**Common findings:**
- Upgrade to LED bulbs (immediate savings)
- Weatherize windows/doors (3-5% savings)
- Adjust thermostat 2-3 degrees (10% savings)
- Get smart thermostat rebates

**ACTION 3: Enroll in Time-of-Use (TOU) Rates**
> "I want to enroll in your Time-of-Use rate plan. When are off-peak hours, and what's the rate difference?"

**TOU rates often save 5-15% if you shift usage to off-peak (evening/morning):**
- Peak (4pm-9pm): Higher rates
- Off-peak (9pm-7am): Lower rates
- SuperOff-peak (midnight-5am): Lowest rates

**ACTION 4: Natural Gas Efficiency**
> "What programs do you have for natural gas efficiency? Rebates for water heater upgrades? Smart thermostat incentives?"

Common California rebates:
- Water heater replacement: $150-300 rebate
- Smart thermostat: $50-100 rebate
- Weatherization: Up to $1,000 rebate

**SPECIFIC SCRIPT FOR GAS RATE CHALLENGE:**
> "I noticed my natural gas bill increased to $82/month. Last year it was [previous amount]. Can you explain the rate increase? And are there any programs or payment plans that could help reduce this?"

**Expected Outcome:**
